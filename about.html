About Camille

I'm a designer who loves to deliver wonderful solutions that meet business goals.

For over 15 years I’ve worked for product companies and agencies. I'm skilled at wire-framing, prototyping and UX research. Plus, with a background in visual design, I love to create UI or lead UI designers, in cohesion with a design system.

As a deep thinker, I relish solving problems. I have a proven track record of offering fresh ideas and leveraging empathy to craft solutions that resonate with users.

I enjoy fostering collaboration and am trained by Nielsen Norman in conducting workshops. I believe that influence and subtle persuasion is key in order to produce compelling solutions. Combined with my kindess and thoughtfulness, I excel at communication.

I thrive in environments where teams aim to achieve a strategic vision.

I'm based in London.
