Techspace

I conducted UX and UI design for Techspace, a tech-oriented workspace company.

My role was to:

Year

• Conduct research
• Define user experiences
• Create UI for mobile app & web

2018

UX/UI for the mobile app

I designed Techspace's mobile app end-to-end. It enabled workspace occupants to book rooms and spaces, access help information regarding their office, and maintain information for their public profile. It was released in 2018.

Wireframes Flows UI Design

UX/UI for the web

I designed Techspaces' web platform, which helps customers book rooms and spaces and read information regarding their office location. I conducted UX research by interviewing users of the current system and rapidly testing prototypes, and I created final UI with a libary of design assets.

Wireframes Flows UI Design

UI Design assets

I was a permanent employee at Techspace placed as a UX/UI Designer, so part of my job was to create UI assets for the internal engineer team to work from. In 2018 I was using Sketch (prior to Figma) and providing clear specifications.

Design System

UX design process examples

I deployed design thinking methods for Techspace, namely user research, rapid user testing, and creating wireframes and flows.

Discovery research

The first step was to dig into quantitative data, conduct a survey and also do a series of customer interviews to understand behaviour, preferences and processes.

Wireframes and rapid user testing

Early in the design phase I played with ideas using paper sketches. I then translated these to wireframes and explored how to find solutions to user problems in the simplest interface. I created fully interactive prototypes for desktop and mobile and tested these with users by observing how easily they completed tasks.

User flows

I created flows that defined how users would move through interfaces and complete tasks.

Results

The impact of my work on this project, broadly, includes better customer satisfaction and improved business value.
